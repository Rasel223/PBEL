ABOUT US

PARAMOUNT BTrac Energy Limited

Paramount BTrac Energy Ltd (PBEL) is a joint venture between Bangla Trac Ltd & Paramount Textile Ltd, was incorporated as a private limited company on February 25,2018. PBEL was established to operate Independent power Project (IPP) after being awarded of IPP by Bangladesh Power Development Board (BPDB) through a Letter of intent(LOI) dated 27 February 2018. The objective of the JV company is install Hight speed Diesel (HSD) power plant of 200 MW capacity at Baghabari, Sirajganj
